Why testing is important Humans make mistakes, testing helps to discover these mistakes and enables us to mitigate them. Mistakes can cost money and time. REPEATING THE SAME MISTAKES is not acceptable. Testing for those past mistakes helps reduce troubleshooting overhead by eliminating known possibilities of past mistakes. Do the right thing becuase it's the right thing to do. Testing is the right thing.

Pester isnt' just for testing PowerShell / PowerCLI code. You can test infrastructure as well! If PowerShell or PowerCLI can reach it, Pester can test it!
